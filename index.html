About Me

I am a Vietnamese-Canadian, born and raised in the city of Mississauga, Ontario. All throughout highschool, my favourite subject was always math - until I took my first computer science class in 11th grade! I instantly fell in love with the problem solving and creative thinking skills computer science requires, and that is when my coding journey began. Now being a student at Waterloo for computer science, I have learned languages such as C, Java, JavaScript, C++, HTML/CSS, and Racket. I love seeing my code turn into meaningful pieces of work, and I cannot wait to further apply the things I learn as a developer in the tech industry.
